Ito na nga ang unang araw ko naman sa loob ng eskwelahan. Medyo overwhelmed lang. Sa totoo lang, hindi pa ako nakakakain ng tama at hindi ko pa rin alam kung anong gagawin ko. Gumawa na ako ng listahan ng mga dapat bilhin, kailangan ko silang bilhin kaso walang oras sa ngayon. Kailangan ko pa rin hanapin kung saan pwede bumili ng mga supplies pati. Medyo malayo kasi mga bilihan dito.

Never underestimate the power of a good meal. Definitely, made some progress today. For the first time ever since I left the Philippines, I feel excited on what's to come (although I know that feelings are fleeting). But, I take this as a sign that there are good things ahead of this journey. My "thankfulness" that I got here is much more authentic as it's coming from a deeper part of my being.
